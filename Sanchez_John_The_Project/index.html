ABOUT

We are bunch of ordinary folks tryin' to get our run on! Our goal is to run when we can with as many as we can; this may sound ambitious but there really isn't a ton of us so we run with, tops, eight at a time - WHOA BABY! At any given time someone is training for an event, so while we have fun we are always helping, encouraging and cheering for our compadrés. If we can go support someone at an event we will, it's a blast to see a fellow runner crossing the finish line!

Joining is easy, just show up at our next run and you are in, BLAMMO!
